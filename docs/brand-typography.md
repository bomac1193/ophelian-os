# Brand Typography

## Default Pairing

| Role | Font |
|------|------|
| Display/Headers | **Söhne** |
| Body/UI | **Inter** |

Clean, systematic, prioritizes structure over warmth. Ruler-dominant.

---

## Typography Options

### Option A: Trust and Neutrality (DEFAULT)

| Role | Font | Character |
|------|------|-----------|
| Headers | Söhne | Architectural, serious, vault-like |
| Body/UI | Inter | Neutral, invisible, trustworthy |

**Effect:** Clean, systematic, prioritizes structure over warmth. Ruler-dominant.

---

### Option B: Trust with Warmth

| Role | Font | Character |
|------|------|-----------|
| Headers | Söhne | Authority without shouting |
| Body | Spectral | Modern serif, warm, legible |

**Effect:** Sans headers with serif body creates typographic contrast. Introduces Lover dimension without compromising Ruler. Warmth in long-form text.

---

### Option C: Presence with Warmth

| Role | Font | Character |
|------|------|-----------|
| Headers | Fraunces | Variable optical serif, crafted, sensual |
| Body/UI | Inter | Neutral, clear, humane |

**Effect:** More "artisanal" feeling. Magician energy in headers. Bigger visual shift from current aesthetic.

---

### Option D: Original Pairing

| Role | Font | Character |
|------|------|-----------|
| Headers | Söhne Brün | Cold, institutional, disciplined |
| Body | IBM Plex Sans | Open-source corporate, documentation feel |

**Effect:** Over-indexes on Ruler. Reads as "ethical tech company." Weak on Lover and Magician.

---

## Typography Principles

From the Operative Manifesto:

> "Headers carry presence. Body carries truth."

- Headers should not explain rights
- Body should not perform mystique
- The Ruler is "visible, boring, and firm — by design"
- Magic lives in content, not typography

---

## Diacritics Test

All fonts must render these correctly:

- **ZÀNÀ** (À)
- **SÉLÒ** (É, Ò)
- **ÒRÍX** (Ò, Í)

Test at multiple sizes: 72px, 48px, 24px, 16px, 12px.
